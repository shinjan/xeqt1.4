Do More With Less

Hello

XeQt is a group of 2nd generation technology developers whose motto, "Do More With Less" drives every decision, design and deployment mechanism of a group of highly skilled engineers. XeQt is based in and operates from Kolkata, India. We are strong promoters of open-source technology, minimalistic designs and the belief that great isn't good enough. With XeQt, you can always expect to be surprised.

Our Vision

A couple of years back, two students, sitting through a boring class at college, had an idea that would be both fun and innovative, and would also shape their lives in the days to come. And XeQt was born out of that idea. From a web design startup, today we have branched into core hardware development, app development, software design, tutorials, social media and marketing and have even bigger plans in the days to come. It is really very exciting, and we can hardly wait to get started.
